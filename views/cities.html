Average Maximum Temperature Variation

Insights

  • According to Bureau of Metrology, minimum temperatures are recorded usually in the afternoon. Hence the above visualization can be used to get the idea of day temperatures for various cities.
  • Looking at the visualization, Brisbane has the hottest days whereas Hobart has the coldest days, compared to other cities.
  • All the cities except Melbourne shows increasing trend in avg max temperature over past two decades.
  • But there is a drastic difference of approximately 8 degree Celsius in avg max Temperature between Hobart and Brisbane. This can be attributed to the location of the cities, as Brisbane is closer to equator.
  • Also, Melbourne has pretty much stable temperature. Because of acclimating nature of its climate. Hence it would be a good choice to reside in Melbourne.
  • But if you are looking for even cooler city, then Hobart is your choice


Average Minimum Temperature Variation

Insights

  • According to Bureau of Metrology, minimum temperatures are recorded overnight. Hence the above visualization can be used to get the idea of night temperatures for various cities.
  • Looking at the avg. minimum temperature, again Brisbane has the highest values whereas Hobart the lowest. Thus, Brisbane has the hottest nights followed by Sydney, whereas Hobart has the coldest nights.
  • The average minimum temperature for Melbourne, Adelaide and Perth range within a difference of a degree.
