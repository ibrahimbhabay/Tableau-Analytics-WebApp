Australia's Temperature Trend Analysis

Insights

  • By looking at the visualization, it can be said that over the period of merely 26 years (1991-2016), the average temperature has increased by approximately 0.5 degree Celsius.
  • Also, if you look at the forecast for next 4 years (2017 - 2020) the temperature has never dropped below 22 degree Celsius. While until 2012 the temperature kept on varying between 21 degree Celsius and 22 degree Celsius.
  • On top of that, the trend runs from below 22 degree Celsius to above 22 degree Celsius over the years (1991 -2020).
  • Considering all these factors it can be concluded the average temperature of Australia has increased over past 26 years. And will keep on increasing in the future given the trend it is following.
  • One important observation that can be made is that 2013 was the hottest year in past 3 decades. This observation can be corroborated by the article published in The Guardian, confirming that 2013 was hottest year as reported by Bureau of Metrology.

Australia's Temperature Variation Across Seasons

Insights

  • The visualization unequivocally depict that the summers are getting hotter, so are the springs and autumn.
  • Whereas there is a slight decreasing trend for winters.
  • These trends delineate that the warming is evident.
