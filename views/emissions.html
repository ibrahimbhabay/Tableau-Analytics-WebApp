CO2 Emission vs Tempearature

Insights

  • The temperature variation does not fully corelate with CO2 emission. However, rise in CO2 emission causes rise in temperature.
  • For the year 2012 0 2014, though CO2 emission has decreased but temperature is still rising, this is due to delayed effect of CO2 emission, as reported by climate scientists.


Factors Contributing To Rise In Temperature

Insights

  • The CO2 emission is not the only factor responsible for rise in temperature, but other greenhouse gases like Methane, Nitrous oxide HFC, CFC also contribute to the increase of temperature due to greenhouse effect.
  • If we combine all the factors including CO2 emission, we can see an improved correlation between rise in emission of these gases and rise of temperature.
